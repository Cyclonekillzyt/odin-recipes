Spicy Soup

Description

This is a great soup that I got while visiting Houston. It has quickly become a family favorite!.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon unsalted butter
  • ¼ cup chopped celery
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ cups chicken broths
  • 1 ½ cups chopped broccoli
  • 1 ½ cups cauliflower, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • ¼ cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir celery and garlic until garlic is fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in flour to make a roux; cook for 1 minute.
  3. SStir in chicken broth, broccoli, cauliflower, peanut butter, salt, and red pepper flakes. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Mix in green onions and heavy cream just before serving.
